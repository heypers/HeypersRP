         Game mode
═════════════════════════════
It will help you improved 
the gameplay

•Don't lose your items when you die:
/gamerule keepInventory true

•Fire that does not go out or break blocks:
/gamerule doFireTick false

•Avoid spawn from hostiles mobs:
/gamerule doMobSpawning false

•Select spawn. When you die, you will appear in this place:
/spawnpoint

•Blocks not drop item:
/gamerule doTileDrops false
